Who We Are

Sportskin is India's first end-to-end sportswear consultancy, dedicated to helping brands, manufacturers, athletes and institutions unlock their potential in the activewear and athleisure space.

What We Do

We see a world where every founder has the tools, network, and expertise to build a competitive activewear brand. A world where manufacturers transition seamlessly into high-growth categories, and where India stands at the forefront of the global sportswear value chain.

We also provide strategic consultancy and mentorship to guide early-stage brands and help manufacturers enter the activewear space. Our custom services include private label development, fabric innovation, and OEM/ODM support. Through Sportskin Academy, we equip founders and teams with the skills needed to thrive in this competitive market. We're not just your service provider — we're your growth partner.

About The Founder

Shaping India's Sportswear Future

Mridul Das, with 25+ years in sportswear, played a pivotal role at Future Group and Adidas India, driving a 10X growth in Adidas Kids & Golf. He led Khelo India's national sportswear innovation—impacting thousands of athletes.

In 2023, he launched Sportskin, a first-of-its-kind consultancy helping brands build performance wear through strategy, sustainability, and smart scaling.
