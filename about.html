ABOUT US

Our Journey

From Vision to Industry Transformation

Sportskin began with a simple yet powerful belief — that sportswear in India deserved a revolution. Our founder, Mridul Das, brought over 25 years of deep experience with iconic brands like Adidas and Future Group to create something India had never seen before: an end-to-end sportswear consultancy.

From leading national innovation for Khelo India to transforming grassroots apparel accessibility, Mridul's journey has always focused on making performance wear more inclusive and effective. In 2023, Sportskin was launched to bring strategic insight, sustainable sourcing, and design excellence to startups, manufacturers, and global brands entering the Indian market.

Today, Sportskin is not just a company; it's a growing movement of creators, innovators, and entrepreneurs building the future of activewear—one brand, one product, and one athlete at a time.

Our Vision

To Lead India's Sportswear Revolution

We envision a future where India becomes a global hub for innovative, sustainable, and high-performance sportswear. Sportskin aims to be the driving force behind this transformation by serving as a trusted partner to brands, manufacturers, and institutions.

We see a world where every founder has the tools, network, and expertise to build a competitive activewear brand. A world where manufacturers transition seamlessly into high-growth categories, and where India stands at the forefront of the global sportswear value chain.

Our Mission

Building the Next Sportswear Leaders

Our mission is to enable and empower at least 100 dynamic sportswear players—startups, manufacturers, and established global brands—over the next five transformative, high-growth years through strategy, innovation, sustainability, and mentorship.

We strive to democratize access to world-class performance wear solutions by offering customized guidance across every stage of brand and product development. From concept to consumer, we exist to help you move with purpose, design with impact, and grow with confidence.

What We Do
