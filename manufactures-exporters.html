MANUFACTURERS & EXPORTERS

Start Strong Scale Smart

You've built a strong foundation in garment manufacturing. Now it's time to grow where the demand is headed: sportswear and athleisure.

At Sportskin, we partner with manufacturers and export units ready to enter high-growth activewear categories. We help you transform your production capabilities, align with global buyer expectations, and confidently develop performance-ready collections. Whether you're new to sportswear or scaling your offering, our proven process supports capability building, product strategy, and client acquisition.

HOW WE WORK

How We Help Manufacturers Succeed

1

Diagnostic Assessment

We start with a technical deep-dive to evaluate your team's current capabilities, challenges, infrastructure, and activewear readiness.

2

Category & Product Planning

We help you select the right product categories — gymwear, yoga, running, teamwear, or athleisure — based on machinery, fabric access, and buyer potential.

3

Resource Allocation & Team Training

We guide your internal team (merchandising, sampling, QA) through workshops on construction, fit, finish, and functional material use.

4

Range Development Support

We help develop a smart, buyer-ready sample line with production-friendly SKUs, pricing guidance, and seasonal flow.

5

Vendor + Fabric Strategy

Tap into our network of technical fabric suppliers and trim vendors aligned to performance standards.

6

Buyer Presentation & Export Prep

We assist with pitch kits, product catalogs, and B2B outreach strategies to help you generate qualified leads and attend trade shows with confidence.

Why Sportswear Now?

Global demand for sportswear and athleisure is accelerating — across D2C brands, marketplaces, and private label buyers. Traditional woven/knit manufacturers often lack the specific knowledge, range strategy, or material access required for this category.

Sportskin helps you fill those gaps — with deep industry expertise, vendor-ready guidance, and hands-on team training.
