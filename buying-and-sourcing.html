Design & Sampling

Smart Sourcing. Sustainable Results.

At Sportskin, we help sportswear brands and manufacturers source the right materials, vendors, and production partners to bring high-performance products to life. Our Buying & Sourcing service connects you with a curated network of ethical, compliant, and technically capable suppliers across India and beyond — ensuring your sourcing strategy aligns with your quality, sustainability, and pricing goals.

From technical fabrics and trims to production-ready vendor partnerships, we handle everything from requirement analysis to final shipment coordination. Whether you're launching a new line or scaling production, we simplify the complexities of activewear sourcing with speed, transparency, and expertise.
