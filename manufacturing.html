Manufacturing

Precision Production for Performance Wear

At Sportskin, we offer end-to-end manufacturing solutions tailored for the evolving needs of sportswear and athleisure brands. Whether you're producing your first capsule collection or scaling into bulk orders, our expertise ensures your products are made with precision, consistency, and performance in mind.

We partner with certified and category-specific factories across India, helping you deliver functional, high-quality garments that are built to last. From pre-production to post-shipment — every stage is quality-checked and timeline-aligned.

What We Offer

Smart Factory Onboarding

We connect you with vetted manufacturing partners based on your product category (gymwear, yoga, running, athleisure) and production goals. Each partner is evaluated on compliance, technical capabilities, and quality processes.

Production Planning & Management

We manage the complete production lifecycle — from BOM (Bill of Materials) and PO (Purchase Order) handover to development timelines and pre-production sample approvals.

Pre-Production Sign-Offs

Our team assists in fit analysis, sample approvals, and locking performance standards before bulk manufacturing begins.

Bulk Manufacturing & Quality Control

From inline audits to final quality checks and packing validations, we ensure every batch meets brand expectations and functional standards.

Logistics & Coordination

We oversee dispatch, documentation, and post-shipment follow-ups — ensuring smooth delivery and factory-to-market alignment.
